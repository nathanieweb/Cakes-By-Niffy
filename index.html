Founder of Niffycakes

Hi, I’m Nifemi, the founder of Cakesbyniffy! My love for baking started as child when i was in Jss3 class, i used to help this street sister of mine do cleaning whenever she was baking, and i would beg mum to allow me stay with her when she’s decorating the cakes I proceeded to aquire a physical class on cake baking and decoration, pastries making and cake tutoring, eventually attended 4 different cake schools I studied Mass Communication, had my NYSC in Sokoto state, and came back to my bakery to continue my baking business.
